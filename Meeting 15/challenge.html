Charles Leclerc

Description: Charles Leclerc, Prince of Monaco, Driver for scuderia Ferrari
Age:27

  • Charles Leclerc is a Formula 1 driver for Scuderia Ferrari, having made his debut in 2018 with Sauber.
  • He has won eight Grands Prix and finished as runner-up in the 2022 World Drivers' Championship.
  • Leclerc is multilingual, speaking French, Italian, and English fluently.
